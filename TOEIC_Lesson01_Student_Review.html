今日重點總覽

英文句子的核心不是硬背五大句型，而是先看 動詞後面缺什麼。
不及物動詞
後面不用受詞。
The price rose.
及物動詞
後面需要受詞。
She bought a ticket.
補語
不是動作對象，而是補充主詞或受詞。
The room looks clean.
雙受詞
常見意思是「給誰＋給什麼」。
I gave Mary a book.

一、及物動詞與不及物動詞

1. 不及物動詞 Intransitive Verb

不及物動詞後面不需要受詞，句子就可以完整。

常見不及物動詞搭配 / 例句
arrive 到達arrive at the airport
注意：arrive the airport 是錯的。
happen / occur 發生The accident happened yesterday.
rise 上升The price rose.
listen 聽listen to music
respond 回應respond to an email
participate 參加participate in a meeting

2. 及物動詞 Transitive Verb

及物動詞後面需要受詞，句子才完整。

常見及物動詞正確搭配常見錯誤
discuss 討論discuss the issuediscuss about the issue
attend 參加attend a meetingattend to a meeting
enter 進入enter the roomenter into the room
contact 聯絡contact the customercontact with the customer
answer 回答answer the questionanswer to the question

二、五大句型

句型核心概念例句
S + V動詞自己完整The price rose.
S + V + O動詞需要受詞She bought a ticket.
S + V + SC補充說明主詞The room looks clean.
S + V + O + C補充說明受詞The news made him nervous.
S + V + O + O給誰＋給什麼She sent her boss an email.
判斷順序：先找動詞 → 看後面有沒有受詞 → 判斷後面的字是在補主詞還是補受詞。

三、TOEIC Reading Part 5｜101–120 題目解析

101. Our company plans to ______ more employees during the busy holiday season.

(A) take on　(B) wait on　(C) move to　(D) apply for

答案：A take on

解題：plan to + 原形動詞。後面是 more employees，所以要選「雇用員工」：take on employees。

選項：take on = 雇用 / 承擔；wait on customers = 服務顧客；move to = 搬到；apply for = 申請。

例句：The company will take on ten new employees.

102. The department store ______ for three days next week in order to take stock.

(A) has closed　(B) will close　(C) be closed　(D) was closing

答案：B will close

解題：next week 是未來時間，用 will + V。

補充：close 可當不及物動詞，The store will close. = 店會關門。

生詞：department store 百貨公司；take stock 盤點庫存；in order to 為了。

103. Our new juice products were selling like ______ at the end of last quarter.

(A) hotcakes　(B) hot goods　(C) white goods　(D) red tape

答案：A hotcakes

解題：sell like hotcakes = 熱銷。

選項：white goods = 大型家電；red tape = 繁文縟節。

例句：The new phone is selling like hotcakes.

104. The most common source of dietary problems while traveling is ______ water.

(A) drinking　(B) drink　(C) drank　(D) drunk

答案：A drinking

解題：空格修飾 water，drinking water 是「飲用水」。這裡 drinking 不是正在喝，而是形成名詞片語。

生詞：source 來源；dietary 飲食的；while traveling 旅行時。

105. ______ most managers, one common cause of problems in decision-making is over-optimism.

(A) In　(B) When　(C) By　(D) For

答案：A In

解題：句意是「在大多數經理身上」，問題傾向存在於某群人身上，用 in。

比較：For most managers = 對大多數經理而言；這題不是觀點，而是問題存在於他們身上。

生詞：decision-making 決策；over-optimism 過度樂觀。

106. People should arrange their daily schedules appropriately to include a healthy balance ______ work and leisure.

(A) relation　(B) between　(C) to　(D) linking

答案：B between

解題：固定搭配：a balance between A and B。

例句：It is important to maintain a balance between work and family.

生詞：arrange 安排；appropriately 適當地；leisure 休閒。

107. Recycling is one of many ______ ways to care for our environment.

(A) additional　(B) impractical　(C) unworkable　(D) effective

答案：D effective

解題：空格修飾 ways，需要形容詞；回收是保護環境的有效方法。

選項：additional 額外的；impractical 不實際的；unworkable 不可行的；effective 有效的。

108. Nowadays, with the vast popularity of email, ______ people communicate through letter writing.

(A) less　(B) more　(C) most　(D) unless

答案：A less

解題：email 普及，所以寫信的人變少。標準文法 people 可數常用 fewer，但選項沒有 fewer，因此選 less。

生詞：vast popularity 廣泛普及；communicate through 透過……溝通。

109. A good designer usually ______ sharp observation and a good imagination.

(A) has　(B) have　(C) had　(D) has been

答案：A has

解題：主詞 A good designer 是單數，usually 表示習慣，用現在簡單式第三人稱單數 has。

生詞：sharp observation 敏銳觀察力；imagination 想像力。

110. Nowadays, many parents are too busy ______ spend a sufficient amount of quality time with their children.

(A) than　(B) as well　(C) too　(D) to

答案：D to

解題：固定句型：too + 形容詞 + to V，太……以至於不能……。

例句：I am too tired to study.

生詞：sufficient 足夠的；quality time 高品質相處時間。

111. A person’s appearance creates the first impression, but a good personality ______ more in the long run.

(A) matter-of-fact　(B) matters　(C) matter　(D) doesn’t matter

答案：B matters

解題：a good personality 是單數主詞，matter 當動詞是「重要」，所以用 matters。

補充：matter-of-fact = 實事求是的；in the long run = 長遠來看。

112. In this competitive society, people are ______ a lot of stress.

(A) gathering　(B) under　(C) exposed　(D) welcoming

答案：B under

解題：固定搭配：be under stress = 承受壓力。

注意：exposed 要搭配 to：be exposed to stress。

113. To learn something successfully requires not only memorization ______ comprehension of facts.

(A) but also　(B) follow by　(C) as long as　(D) along with

答案：A but also

解題：固定句型：not only A but also B。

例句：The job requires not only experience but also patience.

生詞：memorization 記憶；comprehension 理解。

114. Marketing people need to know how advertising and packaging ______ people’s shopping decisions.

(A) influenced　(B) influencing　(C) influence　(D) influential

答案：C influence

解題：how 後面是名詞子句，主詞 advertising and packaging 是兩個並列概念，所以用原形動詞 influence。

詞性：influence 可當動詞/名詞；influential 是形容詞「有影響力的」。

115. Team work always helps in increasing workplace ______.

(A) productivity　(B) products　(C) producing　(D) produces

答案：A productivity

解題：workplace productivity = 職場生產力，是固定常見搭配。

詞性：productivity 名詞；product 產品；produce 動詞；producing V-ing。

116. Soda ______ coffee are the beverages most commonly preferred by people in the US.

(A) or　(B) and　(C) both　(D) too

答案：B and

解題：Soda and coffee 兩個名詞並列。若用 both，應該是 Both soda and coffee。

生詞：beverage 飲料；prefer 偏好；commonly 普遍地。

117. Employees are ______ when they can get additional learning opportunities sponsored by the employer.

(A) depressed　(B) deprived　(C) delighted　(D) despondent

答案：C delighted

解題：得到額外學習機會是正面情緒，所以選 delighted。

選項：depressed 沮喪的；deprived 被剝奪的；despondent 灰心的。

118. My boss always reminds us that time is something that should be ______ and spent productively.

(A) treasured　(B) treasure　(C) treasurer　(D) treasuring

答案：A treasured

解題：should be + p.p. 被動語態。time should be treasured = 時間應該被珍惜。

詞性：treasure 動詞/名詞；treasurer 財務主管；treasuring V-ing。

119. This museum ______ extensions of international art from all over the world.

(A) accommodates　(B) evaluates　(C) simplifies　(D) accomplishes

答案：A accommodates

解題：museum 可以容納、展示藝術作品，所以選 accommodates。

選項：evaluate 評估；simplify 簡化；accomplish 完成。

注意：extensions 這裡用字不太自然，常見會是 exhibitions 或 collections，但選項中 A 最合理。

120. In general, people just read the newspaper to keep ______ on current events.

(A) state-of-the-art　(B) outdated　(C) old-fashioned　(D) up-to-date

答案：D up-to-date

解題：固定片語：keep up-to-date on current events = 掌握時事。

選項：state-of-the-art 最先進的；outdated 過時的；old-fashioned 老派的。
